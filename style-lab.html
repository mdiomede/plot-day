Style lab 🎨

One vignette, three owned directions — same shapes, different soul. All pure code.

A · Soft gouache

No outlines anywhere — shapes separate by tone, like opaque paint. Irregular blobs, one warm light source, and a paper-grain wash that unifies every element. Cozy picture-book energy.

B · Storybook ink

One warm ink line around everything — but wobbled by a hand-tremor filter so no line is ruler-straight, with sketchy interior detail strokes. Reads as drawn, not generated. Quentin Blake meets seed catalog.

C · Cut paper

No lines, no grain — depth does all the work. Every shape is a paper layer with a soft real shadow, stacked like a collage. Tactile and modern; the whole board would feel like a diorama you reach into.
